Karma helps you spend more time on teaching and less on coding.

It takes too much time and effort to produce educational software. Even worse, most development time is spent on the technical side and too little on teaching.

Karma is an open-source JavaScript library and framework that leverages the latest web technologies so you can spend more time teaching and less time engineering.
